🚀 Indore-Bazar AI-Powered Grocery Shopping for the Next-Gen Bharat

Indore-Bazar is our Swiggy Instamart–inspired AI-first ecommerce platform, built right here in Indore (M.P.), designed to give users a smart, instant, and personalized grocery shopping experience.

This app isn’t just a store — it’s your AI grocery assistant. Chat, explore, and shop… all in one smooth flow. Perfect for fast-moving users, busy students, families, and literally anyone who wants groceries ASAP.

✨ Why Indore-Bazar?

Because normal apps show products. We talk to you. We understand your needs, habits, and vibe — and help you pick the perfect items instantly.

💡 What makes it special?

🤖 AI Chatbot Powered Shopping Ask “Bhai, healthy breakfast ke liye kya lu?” and get instant curated ₹-wise suggestions.

⚡ Ultra-fast UI inspired by Instamart Smooth, modern, Gen-Z level aesthetic.
